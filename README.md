# Averom

> AI supplier risk intelligence platform for verifying suppliers and reducing sourcing fraud.
>
> 🌐 **Live Demo:** [averom.co](https://averom.co)
>
> ---
>
> ## Problem
>
> Businesses sourcing products or services from suppliers face significant risks: fraud, unreliable partners, and costly due diligence processes. Traditional supplier verification is slow, expensive, and often incomplete — leaving companies exposed to financial loss and operational disruption.
>
> ---
>
> ## Solution
>
> Averom is a B2B SaaS platform that uses AI to automate supplier risk intelligence. It empowers procurement teams and founders to verify suppliers instantly, assess risk profiles, and make confident sourcing decisions — all in one place.
>
> ---
>
> ## How It Works
>
> 1. **Submit a Supplier** — Enter a supplier's name, domain, or registration details.
> 2. 2. **AI Analysis** — Averom's AI engine cross-references multiple data sources to build a risk profile.
>    3. 3. **Risk Scoring** — Each supplier receives a risk score based on financial stability, fraud signals, and compliance indicators.
>       4. 4. **Review & Act** — Users receive a detailed report with actionable insights and recommendations.
>          5. 5. **Monitor Over Time** — Continuous monitoring alerts you to changes in supplier risk status.
>
